Pottery

Pottery is one of the oldest and most widespread forms of craft, known for its beauty and utility. It consists of everyday items like vessels, pots, bowls and plates; generally made from clay and hardened with heat. It also provides therapeutic value to the ones practicing it since it involves creativity and use of hands to shape the art.

Hand Block Printing

Hand block printing in India is a traditional and intricate textile printing technique that involves the use of wooden blocks to create intricate patterns on fabric. Many hand block prints feature traditional Indian motifs such as paisleys, flowers, animals, and geometric patterns, reflecting the rich cultural heritage.
